HEX A.I. Labs:

Pioneering Semiconductor Reliability with AI


Hex A.I. Labs develops an advanced AI-driven platform that revolutionizes semiconductor reliability prediction, transforming the industry’s paradigm. Our proprietary “Semiconductor Gene Analysis” technology accurately forecasts the reliability of machinery and manufacturing processes, empowering companies to make swift, informed decisions.


Inspired by the stable and efficient honeycomb structure, Hex embodies six core commitments: accuracy, speed, reliability, proactiveness, prevention, and user-friendliness. These values drive our innovative solutions, enabling semiconductor companies to eliminate uncertainty, reduce costs, and maximize productivity.


Hex A.I. Labs is shaping the future of the semiconductor industry through cutting-edge AI and data technologies, paving the way for a smarter, more reliable tomorrow.


Columbus: AI-Powered Data Augmentation

Columbus is an AI model trained with minimal wafer data in the ALD process, leveraging our advanced data augmentation algorithm. By inputting ALD process parameters, Columbus predicts oxide properties with precision, utilizing its deep understanding of ALD growth mechanisms and precursor chemistry.
